# Memo: Hybrid Workout Strategy (Home + Gym)

## Objective
Transition to a more flexible workout structure that favors home sessions while maintaining 3-4 high-impact gym sessions per week. This plan leverages existing home equipment (Erg, Rings, Dumbbells) to ensure hypertrophy goals are met regardless of location.

## Equipment "Toolbox"

| Category | Home (Rings, DBs, Erg) | Gym (Gold's) |
|----------|------------------------|--------------|
| **Vertical Push** | DB Overhead Press, Ring Handstand Pushup (progression) | BB Overhead Press, Machine Press |
| **Horizontal Push** | Ring Dips, Ring Pushups, DB Bench | BB Bench Press, Cable Flyes |
| **Vertical Pull** | Pull-ups (Rings), Chin-ups | Lat Pulldowns, Weighted Pull-ups |
| **Horizontal Pull** | Ring Rows, DB Rows | Barbell Rows, Cable Rows, Chest-Supported Rows |
| **Isolation** | DB Curls, DB Extensions, Lateral Raises | Cable Curls, Tricep Pushdowns, Face Pulls |
| **Cardio/HIIT** | Erg (Rowing), Running | Erg, Air Bike, Treadmill |

---

## Proposed Hybrid Split Options

### Option A: The "Big Rocks" Hybrid (4 Gym / 3 Home)
*Best for maximizing hypertrophy on specific days while keeping cardio at home.*

*   **Mon (Gym):** Heavy Push (BB Bench, OHP, Cables)
*   **Tue (Home):** Zone 2 Erg (45 min) + Mobility
*   **Wed (Gym):** Heavy Pull (BB Rows, Pull-ups, Accessories)
*   **Thu (Home):** Hypertrophy "Gap Filler" (Ring Dips, DB Curls, Lateral Raises)
*   **Fri (Gym):** Leg Day or Full Body Hypertrophy
*   **Sat (Home):** Zone 2/HIIT Erg or Run
*   **Sun (Home):** Active Recovery / Rings Skills

### Option B: The "Aesthetic Pivot" (3 Gym / 4 Home)
*Best for the "Road to Reunions" goal. Focuses on gym for heavy compounds and home for high-volume ring/DB work.*

*   **Mon (Gym):** Heavy Push + Shoulders (BB Bench, Heavy DB OHP)
*   **Tue (Home):** Pull-ups + Ring Rows + DB Curls (Hypertrophy Pull)
*   **Wed (Gym):** Heavy Pull + Back Thickness (BB Rows, Deadlifts/Legs)
*   **Thu (Home):** Zone 2 Erg (45 min)
*   **Fri (Gym):** Push/Pull Accessory (Cables, Machines, Vanity Work)
*   **Sat (Home):** Ring Dips + Ring Pushups + DB Lateral Raises (Hypertrophy Push)
*   **Sun (Home):** Long Zone 2 (Erg or Run)

## Strategy for Home Hypertrophy

To ensure home workouts are as effective as gym workouts:

1.  **Rings are the Secret Weapon:** Ring dips and ring pushups offer a level of instability and pectoral contraction that often exceeds fixed bars/dumbbells.
2.  **Volume & Tempo:** At home, if you run out of DB weight, use slower eccentrics (3-4 seconds down) and shorter rest periods (60s) to maintain intensity.
3.  **The Erg for Legs:** While not a substitute for squats, high-intensity rowing intervals can provide significant lower body stimulus if you focus on the leg drive.

## Transition Plan
1.  **Audit DB Weights:** Check if current DBs are heavy enough for primary presses. If not, pivot those movements to the gym or use Rings.
2.  **Setup Rings:** Ensure rings are at a height that allows for both pull-ups and dips/rows comfortably.
