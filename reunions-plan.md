# Road to Reunions Workout Plan

**Goal:** Aesthetics + health for Princeton Reunions (May 21-24, 2026)
**Start date:** April 2, 2026
**Duration:** 49 days / 7 weeks
**Companion app:** `reunions-workout.jsx`

---

## Constraint: Post-Perianal Surgery (1 week post-op as of April 2)

- Avoid intra-abdominal pressure (heavy Valsalva bracing, heavy squats/deadlifts)
- Avoid prolonged sitting on hard surfaces (some machines, bike)
- Upper body muscles (shoulders, arms, chest) are fully trainable via cables/machines
- User can stop anytime — workouts have natural stopping points but allow max effort where safe

## Three-Tier System

Every workout has three tiers with stopping points:

- **GO HARD** — cables, machines, pure isolation. Zero core bracing, zero pelvic floor load. Max effort from day 1.
- **IF FEELING GOOD** — slightly more core engagement (light DB pressing, standing work). Natural exit point if anything feels off.
- **WEEKS 3+ ADD** — free weight compounds, leg work, heavier pulling. Unlock when healing comfort score trends 8+.

Key insight: shoulders, arms, and chest are 90% of the "looks great in clothes" equation, and all trainable at full intensity on cables/machines right now.

## Three Phases

1. **REBUILD (Weeks 1-2):** Max effort upper body via cables/machines. Zero heavy bracing. Healing + habit.
2. **RAMP (Weeks 3-5):** Add free weights, reintroduce legs gradually, push volume.
3. **PEAK (Weeks 6-7):** Full intensity where tolerated. Pump work. Arrive full, not flat.

## Split: PPL x2 + Conditioning (6 on, 1 active recovery)

Shoulder/arm emphasis for the "polo shirt V-taper" look.

1. Push A (Chest & Shoulders)
2. Pull A (Back Width & Biceps)
3. Arms & Delts (Vanity Day)
4. Push B (Shoulder Emphasis)
5. Pull B (Back Thickness & Arms)
6. Pump + Cardio (Conditioning & Weak Points)
7. Rest (Active Recovery)

## Iteration Protocol

Weekly check-in every Sunday morning (post-bathroom, pre-food):

**Metrics:**
- Weight (lbs)
- Waist (inches)
- Shoulders (inches)
- Energy (1-10)
- Healing comfort (1-10)
- Notes / what to adjust

**Key ratio:** Shoulders / waist — target >1.45

**Decision rules:**
- Waist down + weight stable = recomp working, stay course
- Weight dropping >2 lbs/wk = add 200 cal to preserve muscle
- Nothing moving = cut 200 cal or add 10 min walking
- Healing comfort consistently 8+ = unlock blue tier (WEEKS 3+ exercises)

## Nutrition

- **Protein:** 1g/lb minimum. Non-negotiable.
- **Calories:** Slight deficit (200-300 below TDEE). Recomp zone.
- **Fiber:** Extra important post-surgery. 30g+ daily, plenty of water.
- **Creatine:** 5g/day.

## Philosophy

- Don't be too conservative — user can stop anytime, so gear workouts to allow max effort where safe
- Iterative: seed with a good guess, measure weekly, improve based on data
- The program is a living document — swap exercises, adjust volume, troubleshoot plateaus based on check-in data
