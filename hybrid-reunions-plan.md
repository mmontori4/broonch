## Detailed Session Breakdowns

### MONDAY: Heavy Push + Width (Gym)
*Goal: Build the base and initiate the weekly shoulder volume.*
0. **Warmup:** 10 min Zone 2 (Erg or Walk)
1. **BB Bench Press:** 3 x 5-8
2. **Seated DB OHP:** 3 x 8-10
3. **Incline DB Press:** 3 x 10-12
4. **Cable Lateral Raise:** 4 x 15
5. **Tricep Cable Pushdown:** 3 x 12-15
6. **Cooldown:** 5 min Zone 2

### TUESDAY: Lat Width + Biceps (Home)
*Goal: High-frequency vertical pulling and lat stretching.*
0. **Warmup:** 10 min Zone 2 (Erg)
1. **Ring Pull-ups:** 4 x Max
2. **Ring Rows (Feet Elevated):** 3 x 12
3. **DB Lat Pullovers:** 3 x 12-15
4. **DB Curls / Hammer Curls:** 3 x 12 each
5. **Cooldown:** 5 min Zone 2

### WEDNESDAY: Back Thickness + Legs (Gym)
*Goal: Mid-back density + Systemic Health/Strength.*
0. **Warmup:** 10 min Zone 2 (Erg)
1. **Barbell Row (Strict):** 4 x 6-8
2. **Chest-Supported Row:** 3 x 10-12
3. **DB Split Squats:** 3 x 10/side
4. **DB Single-Leg RDL:** 3 x 12/side
5. **Machine Lateral Raise:** 3 x 15
6. **Cooldown:** 5 min Zone 2

### THURSDAY: Erg + Side Delt "Micro-Loading" (Home)
*Goal: Conditioning + frequency advantage for shoulder width.*
1. **Zone 2 Erg:** 60 minutes (Talk test intensity)
2. **DB Lateral Raise (Mini-Session):** 5 sets of 15-20 reps
3. **VO2 Max Protocol (STARTING WEEK 5 - MAY 4):**
   - Add a "Norwegian 4x4" after the Zone 2 session.
   - 4 min at 90% HR / 3 min easy recovery (x4).
   - *Safety: No breath-holding; focus on fluid breathing.*

### FRIDAY: V-Taper Specialization (Gym)
*Goal: High volume for the "V" muscles.*
0. **Warmup:** 10 min Zone 2
1. **Weighted Pull-ups:** 4 x 6-10
2. **Lat Pulldown (Wide Grip):** 3 x 12-15
3. **Cable Lateral Raise (Behind Back):** 4 x 15
4. **Low-to-High Cable Fly:** 3 x 15
5. **Arm Accessories:** 3 sets Curls / 3 sets Extensions
6. **Cooldown:** 5 min Zone 2

### SATURDAY: Ring Hypertrophy Push (Home)
*Goal: Chest and shoulder stimulus via high-intensity ring work.*
0. **Warmup:** 10 min Zone 2
1. **Ring Dips:** 4 x 8-12
2. **Ring Pushups:** 3 x Max
3. **DB Lateral Raise:** 4 x 15-20
4. **Cooldown:** 5 min Zone 2

## The Audit: Why This Works
- **Side Delt Volume:** ~23 sets/week (Hyper-specialization).
- **Lat Volume:** ~18 sets/week (Width focus).
- **Cardio:** ~210 min Zone 2 (Mitochondrial base) + 1x VO2 Max (Starting Week 5).
- **Safety:** Unilateral legs and "breathed-through" cardio avoid intra-abdominal pressure.

## Final Reminders
- **Tempo:** On the Rings and Pullovers, use a 3-second eccentric (downward phase) to maximize muscle damage and growth.
- **RPE:** Aim for RPE 8-9 (leaving 1-2 reps in the tank) for compound lifts, and RPE 10 (failure) for side delt lateral raises.
